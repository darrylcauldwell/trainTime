The smartest way to track UK trains

trainTime is a powerful, beautifully designed app for tracking live UK train departures and monitoring your journey in real-time. Whether you're commuting daily or planning a trip, trainTime keeps you informed with accurate, up-to-the-minute information from National Rail.

KEY FEATURES

Live Train Tracking
• Real-time departure boards filtered by your destination
• See platform numbers, delays, and cancellations instantly
• Track train progress with live position on an interactive map
• Estimated arrival times at every calling point

Live Activities
• Monitor your journey on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island
• See current station, next stop, and live ETA at a glance
• Automatic updates every 30 seconds while your train is moving
• Perfect for keeping track without opening the app

CarPlay Integration
• View your saved journey's next departures on your car display
• Auto-refreshes every 30 seconds for the latest information
• Perfect for checking train times while driving to the station

Home Screen Widget
• Add a widget showing the next departure for your saved journey
• Small and medium widget sizes available
• Updates every 15 minutes automatically

Journey Search by Route
• Search by destination, not just station - see only relevant trains
• Save favourite journeys for instant access
• Recent journey history for quick re-searching
• Get directions to the station using Apple Maps

Offline Support
• View cached departure data when offline
• Clearly marked with last update time
• Automatically refreshes when connection returns

Smart Features
• Find alternative services when your train is cancelled
• Haptic feedback for better interaction
• Auto-refresh keeps departure boards up to date
• View full journey schedules and calling points

Multi-Language Support
• Available in English, Welsh (Cymraeg), French (Français), German (Deutsch), and Spanish (Español)
• Accessible with full VoiceOver support
• Dynamic Type for adjustable text sizes

Beautiful Glass Design
• Modern, translucent interface inspired by iOS design language
• Dark mode optimized
• Smooth animations and intuitive navigation

Data & Privacy
• Live data from National Rail Darwin OpenLDBWS
• No tracking, no ads, no data collection
• Your journeys are stored locally on your device only

trainTime is perfect for commuters, travellers, and anyone using UK rail services. Never miss your train again with real-time notifications and live journey tracking.
